Quesadillas

The quickest thing to make.
Toddlers and Gamers both love them!
Taco Bell eat your heart out.

    Ingredients

  • 1 Corn or Flour Tortilla
  • 1 Handful of Queso Fresco,
    Queso Panella, or Mozzerella Cheese
  • Comal or Griddle

    Directions

  1. Heat tortilla on comal/griddle
    over medium heat, until flimsy.
  2. Cover half of tortilla with cheese, fold over.
  3. Cook 2 minutes each side,
    or until tortilla begins to brown.
