Quesadillas

The quickest thing to make. Toddlers and Gamers both love them!

If you want to get crazy with it you can use leftover chicken, diced onion, or random green stuff to make it seem fancier.

Taco Bell eat your heart out.

Ingredients

  • 1 Corn or Flour Tortilla
  • 1 Handful of Queso Fresco, Queso Panella, or Mozzerella Cheese
  • Comal or Griddle

Directions

  1. Heat tortilla on comal/griddle over medium heat, until flimsy.
  2. Cover half of tortilla with cheese, fold over.
  3. Cook 2 minutes each side, or until tortilla begins to brown.
